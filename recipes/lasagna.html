Lasagna Recipe


Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish consisting of stacked, alternating layers of pasta sheets and various ingredients, including ragù (meat sauce), béchamel sauce, and cheeses like mozzarella, parmesan, or ricotta


ingredients

  1. 2 teaspoons olive oil

  2. 1 pound ground beef

  3. 1 teaspoon salt

  4. 1 (6 ounce) package no-boil lasagna noodles, broken into large pieces

  5. 1 ½ cups marinara sauce, or to taste

  6. 2 cups fresh spinach

  7. 1 (8 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese

  8. 1 (8 ounce) package soft goat cheese

  9. Enjoy!
